How to add your place to To inhabit is to say here?

1) Find a place

  • • In the right panel, click the search box labeled TYPE YOUR ADDRESS HERE.
  • • Type anything from “Rue Oberkampf, Paris” to “Gràcia, Barcelona” or “Lisbon”.
  • • Press SEARCH. The globe centers on your result.

2) Drop or adjust the pin

  • • A pin will appear at the result.
  • • Drag the pin to fine-tune the spot (street corner, building, park).
  • • Click the map to confirm where you want it.

3) Write your note

  • • In TYPE YOUR NOTE HERE, share a memory, a routine, a feeling—whatever “inhabiting” means to you.
  • Ideas:
  • • Passage: a route you cross, a bus stop, a doorway.
  • • Trace: a mark left behind—shoe scuffs, posters, chalk lines.
  • • Settlement: a room, a bench, a studio, a temporary camp.

4) Submit

  • • Press SUBMIT to add your point.
  • • Your dot now lives on the globe for others to explore.

5) Fix a slip

  • • Click UNDO LAST ENTRY to remove your most recent submission.

Tips for a great entry:

  • • You don’t need a precise address. A neighborhood or nearby landmark works.
  • • Protect privacy. If a place is sensitive (home, friend’s address), drop the pin to the nearest public spot.
  • • Be respectful. No personal data about others; keep it kind and safe for all ages.
  • • Any language is welcome. Write in the language that feels right for the memory.

Troubleshooting:

  • • Search finds nothing? Try a broader term (city first, then zoom in).
  • • Pin won’t move? Zoom in and drag slowly; then click once to set.
  • • Accidentally posted? Use UNDO LAST ENTRY right away.
  • • Text won’t submit? Check you’ve typed something in the note box, then try again.
